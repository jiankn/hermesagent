## Short Answer

Pick Hermes if you want a practical operator that mixes chat, tools, skills, and structured workflows. Pick AutoGPT if you want to experiment with autonomous agent loops and are comfortable shaping more of the system yourself.

## Hermes Strengths

- Better fit for human-in-the-loop operation
- Stronger emphasis on skills and practical tooling
- Easier to reason about bounded workflows

## AutoGPT Strengths

- Good for experimenting with autonomous planning loops
- Broad mindshare in agent experimentation
- Useful when you want to study or customize the autonomy stack deeply

## Where Teams Usually Get Surprised

The real question is not “which one is smarter?” It is “which failure mode hurts less for our workflow?” Hermes tends to be more explicit. AutoGPT tends to be more open-ended.
